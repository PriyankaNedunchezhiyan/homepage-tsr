FAQ's

What is training course duration?

  • This training course is 16 (sixteen) weeks duration.

Is this course DG shipping approved for STCW 2010?

  • This course is DG Shipping Approved course and issues certificate as per STCW 2010.

Is this course residential or non residential?

  • This course is non residential. However Candidates from outstation can avail Hostel facility at additional cost.

Is daily transport is available from CBD belapur railway station?

  • Yes, daily transport is available.
  • Bus starts at 0730 hrs from Belapur for T S Rahaman and leaves at 1730 hrs from T S Rahaman for Belapur.

Is family accommodation available?

  • Yes, it could be arranged. While booking the course, you may register your request for such accommodation.
