Is daily transport is available from CBD belapur railway station?

  • Yes, daily transport is available.

What is IMU?

  • The IMU or Indian Maritime University was formed by an Act of Parliament; the headquarters of the University is at Chennai with its campuses at Mumbai, Kolkata Chennai and Vishakhapatnam. Further information about IMU can be obtained from their website www.imu.tn.nic.in

What is Merchant Navy?

  • Merchant Ships that carry goods, people or render services of a non military nature constitute the merchant navy.
