Playgrounds & Sports Facilities

The Campus boasts of basketball and Volleyball cemented courts. Sports such as athletics, Passing out Parades and other mass sports events are organised on the playground adjacent to the Campus. This Playground could be seen in the aerial view of the campus

https://www.youtube.com/embed/YiZy7aA-ANI

Indoor Games

Pool table, Table Tennis, Chess and Carom boards are available in the Hostels for recreation purposes.

Gymnasium

A fully furnished air conditioned Gymnasium is available in the Campus for a subscription of mere Rs. 300/- per month. A qualified Physical trainer has been specially employed to supervise physical exercise regime of the members.
